Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09/12/2024

1. Introduction

Thank you for using our Sleeper Fantasy Football Data Bot (the "Bot"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our Bot, which allows users to query Sleeper fantasy football data. Please read this privacy policy carefully to understand how we manage your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Queried via Sleeper API

Our Bot allows users to query fantasy football data through the Sleeper API. The data retrieved may include league matchups, player performances, and other publicly available fantasy football data related to the Sleeper platform.

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users of the Bot. All queries and results are handled in real-time without retaining any information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the Sleeper API to retrieve fantasy football data as requested by the user in real-time. No user data is stored, collected, or shared by us.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal data with third parties. All data provided by the Bot is directly retrieved from the Sleeper platform via their publicly available API and returned to the user.

5. Security

Although we do not store any user data, we take appropriate measures to ensure that communications with the Bot are secure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Bot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so that we can promptly delete the information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.
